Who Are We?

A group of young teenagers who are pursing their dreams of being the world's best striker. BlueLock is a programme made by Ego, who also invited 300 potential U18 soccer players in Japan to hop onto his creation. Soccer players are slowly eliminated through matches and different stages, and for those who fail to go onto the next stage, their dreams are crushed right on the spot as their soccer career is entirely destroyed.
